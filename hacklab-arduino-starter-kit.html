Shopping list for Arduino Starter Kit

This list has links to the products in the Partco webshop ( http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/find_us.php) , located near Helsinki.  The components can sometimes be gotten cheaper from e.g. http://www.futurlec.com/ and other places instead, but the delivery times are longer.

Addennum: Check out also http://paeae.com/arduino.html

Arduino basics

  • Arduino board
    • http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?cPath=5&products_id=15335
  • USB A-B cable for connecting the Arduino to your computer
    • http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?products_id=10305
  • Breadboard (Koekytkentälevy in finnish)
    • http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?cPath=1174_1902&products_id=11605
  • Jumper wires for the breadboard
    • http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?cPath=1174_1902&products_id=15423
  • Optional: DC power for running the arduino without having it connected to a computer.
    • Should be 6 to 12 Volts, and have a 2.1 mm DC plug (with center pin positive)
    • E.g. http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?products_id=9571

Simple components to play with

  • LEDs
    • Simple (get a few of these)
      • http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/index.php?cPath=1037_1670
    • RGB (Multicolor led, get one if you want, fun to play with)
      • http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/index.php?cPath=1037_1676
  • Resistors
    • For LEDs: 180 to 470 Ohms are suitable (get one for each LED, or three for each RGB LED)
      • E.g. 220 Ohm http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?cPath=2075_12_1025_1329_1348&products_id=12467
    • For pullup / pulldown for buttons: 1kOhm to 100kOhm are suitable (get about one for each button, and maybe a few extra)
      • E.g. 4.7kOhm http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?cPath=2075_12_1025_1329_1348&products_id=12534
  • Potentiometers (variable resistor) (get one or two of these)
    • E.g. Linear, 10kOhm: http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?cPath=2075_12_1027_1606_1931&products_id=5331
  • Buttons (get one or two of these)
    • Pushbutton: http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?cPath=2065_1046_1719&products_id=7224
    • Switch: http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?cPath=2065_1046_1726&products_id=7286
  • Light sensor (get one or two to play with)
    • Fotoresistor (changes resistance from 1kOhm to 1MOhm depending on light level): http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?products_id=5304
  • LED digit displays (fun to play with, get if you want - get a LED resistor for each pin too)
    • http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/index.php?cPath=1037_1684
  • Beeper (makes a continuous, kind of loud sound / tone.  Get one if you want some beeps)
    • http://www.partco.biz/verkkokauppa/product_info.php?products_id=10850
