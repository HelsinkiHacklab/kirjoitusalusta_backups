work in the application you described.  The old Sarantel Helix did not work
upside down but ours will becasue of its 130 degree view. We have
experience catching the road bounce of the GPS signal from 3 feet under a
car with no skyview and the tracker still worked. Our most famous customer
was Felix Baumgardner the Space Jump guy using two of our antennas on the
back of his helmet, see attached photo.  He did twists, tumbles and still
maintained GPS lock.
