Roland GX24 Vinyl cutter instructions
===========================

This is a precision instrument worth well over 3kEUR, you * will * treat it with care and respect. You will * start * by reading the paper manual that came with the machine, then you * will read* the "Cutting Guide" found on the desktop of the Mac controlling the cutter.

When done you will unload the material and replace the dustcover.

Everything else is covered in the fine manuals better than I could here, ask for hands-on instruction from someone who knows how to handle these machines.

New rolls of material from Ebay are on shelf to the right. 12 inch (300 mm) * 5 ft (1.5 m). Correct cutting force seems to be 80-100 gf. This might vary between rolls.
